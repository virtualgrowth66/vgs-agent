Get Started in 30 Seconds

Paste this one snippet before the </body> tag on your website.

<!-- VGS Agent — AI Sales Agent -->
<script src="voice-engine.js"></script>
<script src="widget.js"
        data-agent-id="your-agent"
        data-primary-color="#6366F1"
        data-auto-detect="true"
        data-voice="true"
        data-tts="true"></script>

✨ The widget is already active on this page — look at the bottom-right corner!
